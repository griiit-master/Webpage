Frequently Asked Questions

How much does fraud recovery cost? ‚ñº
Our standard fee is 25% of the recovered amount for cases under $100,000, and 20% for amounts above that threshold. Initial consultation and case assessment are completely free. For complex international cases requiring extensive legal coordination, additional legal fees may apply, but these are always discussed and approved by you before any action is taken.
What's the chance of recovering my money? ‚ñº
Our overall success rate is 81%, but each case is unique. Factors affecting recovery include: how quickly you report the fraud (within 24-48 hours significantly increases success), whether funds were transferred internationally, type of scam involved, amount of evidence available, and cooperation from financial institutions. Cryptocurrency cases average 75% success rate, while wire fraud cases see 85% success when reported within 72 hours. Contact us immediately for a free case assessment and honest evaluation of your recovery prospects.
How long does the recovery process take? ‚ñº
Recovery timelines vary significantly based on case complexity and fraud type. Domestic wire fraud cases with quick reporting (within 24-48 hours) can often be resolved in 2-4 weeks through bank cooperation and legal holds. Romance scams and investment fraud typically take 3-6 weeks as we trace funds and coordinate with law enforcement. Complex international cryptocurrency fraud involving multiple jurisdictions may take 2-4 months. Cases requiring criminal prosecution can extend 6-12 months. We provide weekly progress updates throughout the investigation and maintain 24/7 communication channels so you're never left wondering about your case status.
Can you recover cryptocurrency that was stolen? ‚ñº
Yes! We specialize in blockchain forensics and cryptocurrency tracing using advanced chain analysis tools. While crypto transactions are irreversible on the blockchain, we can: trace stolen funds through multiple wallet addresses and mixing services, identify perpetrators through on-chain and off-chain intelligence, work with centralized exchanges to freeze accounts when funds are converted to fiat currency, and coordinate with international law enforcement for criminal prosecution. Our success rate for crypto recovery is approximately 75%, highest when reported within 72 hours. We've recovered Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, and various altcoins. Speed is critical - contact us immediately if you've been scammed, and do not confront the scammer as this may cause them to move funds faster.
