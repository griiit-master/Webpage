Our Story

Born From a Desire to Serve

Tumaini Foundation was founded in 2012 by a group of passionate Kenyans who saw firsthand the struggles of disadvantaged communities in Nairobi's informal settlements and rural counties. Today, we serve over 47 communities across Kenya, with a special focus on Nairobi, Kisumu, Nakuru, and coastal regions.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2012

Foundation Established

Registered as a Non-Governmental Organization in Nairobi. First community feeding program launched in Mathare.

2014

Scholarship Program Launched

First cohort of 12 secondary school scholarships awarded. Partnership signed with the Ministry of Education.

2016

Community Centers Built

Two community resource centers opened in Kibera and Kawangware, providing free vocational training.

2018

National Expansion

Programs expanded to Kisumu, Mombasa, and Nakuru. Staff grows to 25 full-time employees.

2020

COVID-19 Relief Response

Distributed food and sanitation supplies to over 8,000 families during the pandemic lockdowns.

2024

2,400+ Lives Impacted

Reached our biggest milestone ‚Äî over 2,400 direct beneficiaries across 47 communities nationwide.
